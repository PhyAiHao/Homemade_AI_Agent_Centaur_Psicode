Four Layers of Memory.

From 100 tokens to unlimited. Ascending cost, ascending scope.

L0

Identity

~100 tokens. Always present. Who you are and core constraints.

L1

Essentials

~800 tokens. Top-15 by importance score. Auto-curated every dream cycle.

L2

On-Demand

~2K tokens. Topic-filtered retrieval. Loaded when the conversation shifts.

L3

Deep Search

Unlimited. Full hybrid vector + TF-IDF search. Verbatim chunks + wiki pages.
